Veggie Fried Rice

Description

Veggie fried rice is a colorful, quick, and nutritious meal loaded with fresh vegetables and aromatic seasonings. It’s perfect for using up leftover rice.

It’s a flexible dish—swap in any veggies you like or add tofu or eggs for protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 cup chopped mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil or vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Green onions, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  3. Add the cooked rice and stir well.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and black pepper, mixing thoroughly.
  5. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.
