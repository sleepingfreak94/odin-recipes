Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

A simple and flavorful Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. Perfect for a quick and elegant meal.

This dish is easy to prepare, uses pantry staples, and tastes delicious with a sprinkle of fresh parsley or parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente. Reserve some pasta water and drain.
  2. In a pan, saut√© garlic in olive oil until golden.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and stir.
  4. Toss in spaghetti and reserved water as needed.
  5. Serve hot with parsley or parmesan if desired.
